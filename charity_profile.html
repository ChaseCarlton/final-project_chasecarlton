Bright Horizons Foundation

The Bright Horizons Foundation is committed to supporting children in need by providing them with a healthy breakfast and a safe environment in which they can engage in physical activities. Our goal is to ensure that children receive the nourishment and care they need to thrive academically and socially.

Requirements

  • Must be atleast 16 years of age
  • Must bring some form of identification
  • Must be wearing appropriate attire(long sleeve plain shirt, plain jeans or khakis)
  • Must have your own form of transportation
